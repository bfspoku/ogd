Changelog - Federal Popular Votes

This changelog lists all changes made to the files in the dataset "Echtzeitdaten am Abstimmungstag zu eidgenössischen Abstimmungsvorlagen".

2024-03-03

change
new property at kantone.resultat level: vorlageBeendet is set true when gebietAusgezaehlt is true and the canton has back-confirmed the result.
change
"Résultats anticipés" in Geneva included in the files as an intermediary result on the cantonal level.

2023-06-18

change
new properties at vorlagen level
  • English proposal titles under vorlagenTitel
  • provisorisch: Will be true until vote results are confirmed by the Federal Council (normally 3-4 months after voting Sunday)
  • reserveInfoText: Placeholder for further information on the outcome of the vote, e.g. when a proposal is accepted but will not be implemented because a connected proposal was not accepted. Will be null in most cases

2021-06-13

change
sd-t-17-02-yyyymmdd-eidgAbstimmung.json
File is compressed server-side with gzip format on Sunday

2020-11-29

bugfix
gebietAusgezaehlt at the communal level
Communes of Zurich (261) and Winterthur (230): Results flag is now updated correctly

2020-09-27

change
results at communal level
BS (12) partial results at the communal level as soon as postal votes have been counted
change
results at communal level
Communes of Zurich (261) and Winterthur (230): Results of the Zählkreise are continuously aggregated to partial communal results
